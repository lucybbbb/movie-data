<p>1. Which movies generated the highest box office revenue?

Business question: What are the most commercially successful movies?

Metrics:

Revenue
Revenue ranking
Top 20 movies
2. Which movies were the most profitable?

Business question: Did high revenue actually translate into profit?

Formula:

Profit = Revenue - Budget

Insights:

Top profitable movies
Profit margin %
3. Which genres generate the highest average revenue?

Business question: What type of movie performs best commercially?

Metrics:

Average revenue by genre
Total revenue by genre
4. Which genres provide the best return on investment (ROI)?

Business question: Where should studios invest their money?

Formula:

ROI = ((Revenue - Budget) / Budget) * 100

This is usually more interesting than revenue alone.

5. Do bigger budgets lead to higher revenue?

Business question: Is spending more money worth it?

Analysis:

Budget vs Revenue
Correlation
Scatter plot

Possible finding:

Higher budgets tend to generate higher revenue, but not always.

6. Which directors have the highest average box office performance?

Business question: Which directors consistently deliver successful movies?

Metrics:

Number of movies
Average revenue
Total revenue
Average profit
7. Which actors appear most frequently in successful movies?

Business question: Which actors are associated with commercial success?

Metrics:

Number of appearances
Average revenue
Total revenue generated
8. How has movie production changed over time?

Business question: Are more movies being released each year?

Metrics:

Movies released per year
Growth trend
9. How have genres evolved over time?

Business question: Which genres became more popular across the years?

Metrics:

Movies per genre per year
Genre trends

Example:

Action movies increased significantly after 2000.

10. Which director–genre combinations generate the highest revenue?

Business question: Which creative combinations work best?

Examples:

Director + Genre
Revenue
Profit

Possible finding:

Certain directors consistently outperform within specific genres.

Bonus Questions (great for interviews)
11. Which movies were box-office failures?
Revenue < Budget

Identify:

Biggest losses
Failure rate by genre
12. What is the average budget by genre?

Determine whether some genres require larger investments.

13. Which release years generated the most revenue?

Find the strongest years for the movie industry.

14. Are star actors linked to larger budgets?

Analyze:

Actor appearances
Average movie budget
15. Which genre has the highest profit margin?

Sometimes lower-budget genres outperform blockbusters.
